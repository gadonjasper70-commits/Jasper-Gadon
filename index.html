MEASURE-OPOLY

The Measurement Board Game — Length, Distance, Area & Perimeter

Set Up the Game

Choose 2–4 players. Each starts with $1000. Learn while you play: estimate the length of water tanks, rice storage, and balikbayan boxes, and calculate the area and perimeter of rooms and gardens.

🏆 Goal: Buy properties, collect rent, and answer measurement questions to grow your wealth. The last player who hasn't gone bankrupt wins!
