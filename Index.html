MonoMathGross Income · Deductions · Net Pay

Build and upgrade landmarks across the country, collect payroll wisdom, and become the richest player — by mastering how Filipino workers really get paid.

Player Setup

2–6 players. Give each one a name, a profession token, and mark any AI opponents.

How To Play

Goal: Be the last player who isn't bankrupt.

Turn: Roll two dice, move clockwise, follow the tile you land on. Rolling doubles gives you another roll — three doubles in a row sends you to Jail.

Properties: Buy unowned tiles you land on, or the bank auctions them off. Own a full color set to build houses (red) — evenly across the set — then upgrade 4 houses into a hotel (green).

Payroll Chest: Landing here — or passing GO — means solving a real payroll word problem (Gross Income, Deductions, or Net Pay). Answer correctly to collect the salary/reward; answer incorrectly and you get nothing that turn.

Jail: Pay the fine, roll doubles, or wait it out.

Sound is synthesized in-browser to keep this file lightweight and offline-friendly.
