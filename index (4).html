How To Play

Goal

Be the last player who hasn't exploded.

Your Turn

Play as many action cards from your hand as you like, then draw one card from the deck to end your turn (some cards let you skip the draw or force extra turns onto someone else).

Exploding Equation 💥

If you draw one, you're eliminated immediately — unless you're holding a Function Repair (Defuse) card, which auto-saves you and slides the bomb back into the deck.

Action Cards

⏭️ Skip — end your turn without drawing.
⚔️ Attack — end your turn; the next player must take 2 turns.
🔀 Shuffle — shuffle the draw pile.
🔮 See the Future — privately peek at the top 3 cards of the draw pile.
🤝 Favor — force an opponent to hand you a card of their choice.
🃏 Steal — take a random card from an opponent's hand.
❓ Challenge — force an opponent to answer a Functions question or suffer a penalty.
✋ Nope — cancel another player's action card before it takes effect. Any player holding a Nope card gets a brief window to play it after an action is announced.

Local Multiplayer

This is pass-and-play on one device. When the turn passes to a different human player, a privacy screen appears so opponents don't see each other's hands until they tap "I'm Ready."
